## Safety Features

### Identification Keys
1. **Gill Type**: True gills vs forked ridges (Chanterelle)
2. **Color Change**: Bruising reactions (Bay Bolete → blue, Yellow Stainer → yellow)
3. **Volva**: Cup at base (deadly Amanitas)
4. **Smell**: Pleasant vs chemical odor
5. **Spore Print**: Advanced identification
6. **Habitat**: Tree association matters

### Cooking Requirements
- **MUST COOK**: Bay Bolete (15+ minutes), Wood Blewit
- **Best Young**: Shaggy Ink Cap, Giant Puffball, Beefsteak Fungus
- **Enhanced Dried**: Horn of Plenty, Turkey Tail
