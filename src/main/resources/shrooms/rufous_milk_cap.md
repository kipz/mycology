### Rufous Milkcap
*Lactarius rufus*

**Quick Reference:**
- **Item ID:** `mycology:rufous_milk_cap`
- **Edibility:** Poisonous (requires extensive preparation)
- **Toxicity:** Moderate
- **Season:** Summer to autumn (July-November)
- **Habitat:** Coniferous woodland

**In-Game Properties:**
- **Nutrition:** 0
- **Saturation:** 0.0
- **Effects:** Poison I (400 ticks)

**Identification:**
Reddish-brown milk cap under conifers. Cap 4-10cm, with central umbo, reddish to date-brown. Exudes white milk that doesn't change color. Gills cream to pink-buff. Taste extremely hot and acrid.

Contains irritant compounds. In Scandinavia, prepared by repeated boiling and pickling, but best avoided.
