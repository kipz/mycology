### Butter Cap
*Rhodocollybia butyracea*

**Quick Reference:**
- **Item ID:** `mycology:butter_cap`
- **Edibility:** Edible (poor quality)
- **Toxicity:** None
- **Season:** Autumn (August-December)
- **Habitat:** Woodland

**In-Game Properties:**
- **Nutrition:** 1
- **Saturation:** 0.2
- **Effects:** None

**Identification:**
Small to medium woodland mushroom. Cap 3-7cm, convex to flat, reddish-brown to date-brown, smooth, greasy (buttery) when wet. Gills white to cream, crowded. Stem 5-8cm, paler than cap, tough. Common in mixed woodland.

Edible but poor quality - thin flesh, little flavor.
