### Liberty Cap
*Psilocybe semilanceata*

**Quick Reference:**
- **Item ID:** `mycology:liberty_cap`
- **Edibility:** ILLEGAL (contains controlled substances)
- **Toxicity:** Psychoactive
- **Season:** Autumn (September-December)
- **Habitat:** Grassland, pastures

**In-Game Properties:**
- **Nutrition:** 0
- **Saturation:** 0.0
- **Effects:** Confusion III (1200 ticks), Nausea II (600 ticks), Blindness I (400 ticks)

**Identification:**
**LEGAL WARNING: Liberty Cap contains psilocybin and psilocin, which are Class A controlled substances in the UK. Possession is illegal.**

Liberty Cap is a small grassland mushroom with a distinctive conical cap. The cap is 0.5-2.5cm across, sharply conical to bell-shaped with a pronounced pointed umbo (nipple). The cap never expands flat. The color is pale brown to yellowish-brown when dry, darker olive-brown when moist (hygrophanous), with a separable gelatinous pellicle. The margin has fine radial grooves when moist.

The gills are pale grey becoming purple-brown to purple-black as spores mature, adnate. The stem is 4-10cm tall, very thin (1-3mm), wavy, pale cream to yellowish, often bluish at base. The flesh is thin, cream-colored, bruising blue-green.
