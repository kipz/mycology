### Parrot Waxcap
*Gliophorus psittacinus*

**Quick Reference:**
- **Item ID:** `mycology:parrot_waxcap`
- **Edibility:** Edible (not worthwhile)
- **Toxicity:** None
- **Season:** Autumn (September-December)
- **Habitat:** Grassland

**In-Game Properties:**
- **Nutrition:** 1
- **Saturation:** 0.1
- **Effects:** None

**Identification:**
Small, slimy waxcap with changing colors. Cap 2-5cm, initially bright green (like parrot), fading through yellow to orange or pink. Entire mushroom very slimy. Gills colored like cap, adnexed.

Edible but too small and slimy to be worthwhile.
