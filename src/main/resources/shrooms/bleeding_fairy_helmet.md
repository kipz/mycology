### Bleeding Mycena
*Mycena haematopus*

**Quick Reference:**
- **Item ID:** `mycology:bleeding_fairy_helmet`
- **Edibility:** Inedible
- **Toxicity:** None
- **Season:** Summer to autumn (June-November)
- **Habitat:** Dead wood

**In-Game Properties:**
- **Nutrition:** N/A
- **Saturation:** N/A
- **Effects:** None

**Identification:**
Small mushroom on dead wood. Cap 1-4cm, bell-shaped, reddish-brown. **Diagnostic feature:** Stem exudes dark red "blood" when broken. Gills pale with red edges. Grows in small groups on stumps and logs.

Too small and tough to be edible but interesting for its bleeding characteristic.
