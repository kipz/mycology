### Rooting Shank
*Hymenopellis radicata*

**Quick Reference:**
- **Item ID:** `mycology:rooting_bolete`
- **Edibility:** Inedible
- **Toxicity:** None
- **Season:** Summer to autumn (June-November)
- **Habitat:** Woodland, near tree stumps

**In-Game Properties:**
- **Nutrition:** N/A
- **Saturation:** N/A
- **Effects:** None

**Identification:**
Medium mushroom with very long rooting stem. Cap 3-8cm, convex, pale brown to grey-brown, wrinkled. Gills white, widely spaced. Stem 8-20cm tall (most buried), tapering downward into deep rooting base. Surface wrinkled.

Inedible due to bitter taste and tough texture. The deep rooting stem is diagnostic.
