### Rosy Bonnet
*Mycena rosea*

**Quick Reference:**
- **Item ID:** `mycology:rosy_bonnet`
- **Edibility:** Inedible
- **Toxicity:** None
- **Season:** Summer to autumn (June-October)
- **Habitat:** Woodland floor

**In-Game Properties:**
- **Nutrition:** N/A
- **Saturation:** N/A
- **Effects:** None

**Identification:**
Small pink Mycena. Cap 1-3cm, bell-shaped, pale pink to rose-pink, striated margin. Gills pink to white, adnate. Stem thin, pink, fragile. Grows on woodland floor among leaf litter.

Too small to be worthwhile eating. Pretty color distinctive.
