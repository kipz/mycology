### Grey Knight
*Tricholoma terreum*

**Quick Reference:**
- **Item ID:** `mycology:grey_knight`
- **Edibility:** Edible
- **Toxicity:** None
- **Season:** Autumn (September-December)
- **Habitat:** Coniferous woodland

**In-Game Properties:**
- **Nutrition:** 3
- **Saturation:** 0.4
- **Effects:** None

**Identification:**
Small to medium Tricholoma. Cap 3-8cm, conical to flat, grey with darker fibrous scales. Gills white to grey, sinuate. Stem white to grey, solid. Grows under conifers, especially pine.

Good edible but must be certain - confusion with poisonous Tricholomas possible.
