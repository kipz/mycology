### Funeral Bell / Deadly Skullcap
*Galerina marginata*

**Quick Reference:**
- **Item ID:** `mycology:funeral_bell`
- **Edibility:** DEADLY
- **Toxicity:** LETHAL
- **Season:** Year-round (peak autumn)
- **Habitat:** Dead wood, especially conifers

**In-Game Properties:**
- **Nutrition:** 0
- **Saturation:** 0.0
- **Effects:** Wither II (800 ticks), Poison III (1200 ticks)

**Identification:**
**WARNING: Funeral Bell contains the same deadly amatoxins as Death Cap and has caused fatalities.**

Small brown mushroom growing on wood. Cap 2-4cm, bell to convex, brown to orange-brown, hygrophanous (paler when dry). Gills rust-brown, adnate. Stem 3-8cm, thin, with faint ring zone (often ephemeral). Grows in clusters on dead wood, especially conifer stumps.

**TOXICITY:** Contains amatoxins causing delayed liver/kidney failure. Same symptoms as Death Cap. Often confused with Velvet Shank or Honey Fungus with fatal results.

**Lookalikes:**
- **Velvet Shank** (Flammulina velutipes) - EDIBLE. Black velvety stem base, winter fruiting
- **Honey Fungus** (Armillaria) - Edible with caution. Honey-yellow, substantial ring

**CRITICAL WARNING:** Avoid all small brown mushrooms on wood unless absolutely certain.
