### Trumpet Chanterelle
*Cantharellus tubaeformis*

**Quick Reference:**
- **Item ID:** `mycology:trumpet_chanterelle`
- **Edibility:** Choice Edible
- **Toxicity:** None
- **Season:** Late autumn (September-December)
- **Habitat:** Coniferous and mixed woodland

**In-Game Properties:**
- **Nutrition:** 3
- **Saturation:** 0.5
- **Effects:** None

**Identification:**
Small relative of chanterelle. Cap 2-6cm, funnel-shaped with wavy margin, dark brown to grey-brown. Underside has blunt yellowish ridges (not true gills). Stem hollow, yellow. Fruits late in season.

Excellent edible, similar flavor to chanterelle but more delicate. The hollow stem distinctive.
