### Scarlet Caterpillarclub
*Cordyceps militaris*

**Quick Reference:**
- **Item ID:** `mycology:scarlet_caterpillarclub`
- **Edibility:** Medicinal
- **Toxicity:** None
- **Season:** Autumn (September-November)
- **Habitat:** On buried insect pupae

**In-Game Properties:**
- **Nutrition:** N/A
- **Saturation:** N/A
- **Effects:** None (medicinal)

**Identification:**
Parasitic fungus on insect larvae/pupae. Forms bright orange to scarlet club-shaped fruiting bodies 2-7cm tall emerging from soil. Surface finely roughened. Grows from buried caterpillar or insect pupa.

Used in traditional medicine. Related to expensive Cordyceps sinensis from Tibet. Too small to be significant food source.
