### Spectacular Rustgill
*Gymnopilus junonius*

**Quick Reference:**
- **Item ID:** `mycology:spectacular_rustgill`
- **Edibility:** Poisonous (possibly psychoactive)
- **Toxicity:** Moderate
- **Season:** Summer to autumn (July-November)
- **Habitat:** Dead or living hardwood

**In-Game Properties:**
- **Nutrition:** 0
- **Saturation:** 0.0
- **Effects:** Poison I (400 ticks), Confusion II (800 ticks)

**Identification:**
Large clustered mushrooms on wood. Cap 5-20cm, golden-yellow to orange-brown, dry, scaly. Gills yellow becoming rusty-brown. Stem stout, with ring, yellow. Flesh yellow, very bitter.

Contains unknown toxins, possibly psychoactive compounds. Causes gastric upset. Very bitter taste.
