### Verdigris Agaric
*Stropharia aeruginosa*

**Quick Reference:**
- **Item ID:** `mycology:verdigris_agaric`
- **Edibility:** Inedible (possibly poisonous)
- **Toxicity:** Uncertain
- **Season:** Summer to autumn (June-November)
- **Habitat:** Woodland, grassland

**In-Game Properties:**
- **Nutrition:** 0
- **Saturation:** 0.0
- **Effects:** None

**Identification:**
Striking blue-green mushroom. Cap 3-8cm, bright verdigris blue-green when young (fading to yellow-brown), slimy, with white veil remnants. Gills grey-purple. Stem blue-green with ring.

Edibility uncertain - best avoided. The bright blue-green color is distinctive.
