### Ivory Funnel
*Clitocybe dealbata*

**Quick Reference:**
- **Item ID:** `mycology:ivory_funnel`
- **Edibility:** DEADLY
- **Toxicity:** LETHAL
- **Season:** Summer to autumn (June-November)
- **Habitat:** Grassland, lawns

**In-Game Properties:**
- **Nutrition:** 0
- **Saturation:** 0.0
- **Effects:** Poison III (800 ticks), Confusion II (600 ticks)

**Identification:**
**WARNING: Deadly poisonous - same toxins as Fool's Funnel.**

Small white mushroom. Cap 2-6cm, convex becoming shallowly depressed, white to cream, smooth. Gills white, crowded, decurrent. Stem short, white. Grows in short grassland, often in rings.

Contains high levels of muscarine - potentially fatal.

**Lookalikes:**
- **Fairy Ring Champignon** (Marasmius oreades) - EDIBLE. Tough wiry stem, widely spaced gills, buff color
- Various white grassland mushrooms

**CRITICAL:** Never eat small white mushrooms from grassland.
