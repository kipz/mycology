### Trooping Funnel
*Clitocybe geotropa*

**Quick Reference:**
- **Item ID:** `mycology:trooping_funnel`
- **Edibility:** Edible
- **Toxicity:** None
- **Season:** Autumn (September-December)
- **Habitat:** Woodland edges, grassland

**In-Game Properties:**
- **Nutrition:** 3
- **Saturation:** 0.5
- **Effects:** None

**Identification:**
Large funnel-shaped mushroom growing in troops or rings. Cap 8-20cm, deeply funnel-shaped with prominent central boss, pale buff to ochre. Gills cream, decurrent. Stem tall (8-15cm), robust, pale. Smell pleasant, nutty.

Good edible with pleasant flavor. The large size and central boss distinctive.
