### Dryad's Saddle
*Cerioporus squamosus*

**Quick Reference:**
- **Item ID:** `mycology:dryads_saddle`
- **Edibility:** Edible (young specimens only)
- **Toxicity:** None
- **Season:** Spring to early summer (April-July)
- **Habitat:** Dead or dying hardwood, especially elm and sycamore

**In-Game Properties:**
- **Nutrition:** 2
- **Saturation:** 0.3
- **Effects:** None

**Identification:**
Large bracket fungus. Brackets 10-60cm across, kidney to fan-shaped, cream to yellow-brown with dark brown concentric scales forming saddle pattern. Pores large, hexagonal, cream to yellow. Stem lateral when present. Has distinctive smell like watermelon rind.

Only young, tender specimens are edible - quickly becomes too tough. The watermelon smell is diagnostic.
