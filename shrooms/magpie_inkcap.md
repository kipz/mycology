### Magpie Inkcap
*Coprinopsis picacea*

**Quick Reference:**
- **Item ID:** `mycology:magpie_inkcap`
- **Edibility:** Edible (young only)
- **Toxicity:** None
- **Season:** Summer to autumn (July-October)
- **Habitat:** Woodland, especially beech

**In-Game Properties:**
- **Nutrition:** 2
- **Saturation:** 0.3
- **Effects:** None

**Identification:**
Striking inkcap. Cap 5-10cm when expanded, initially white covered with dark scales creating magpie pattern. Gills white becoming black, deliquescing. Stem white, tall, with basal bulb.

Young specimens edible before deliquescence. Must be cooked soon after picking.
