### Fairy Ring Champignon
*Marasmius oreades*

**Quick Reference:**
- **Item ID:** `mycology:fairy_ring_champignon`
- **Edibility:** Edible
- **Toxicity:** None
- **Season:** Spring to autumn (May-November)
- **Habitat:** Grassland, lawns

**In-Game Properties:**
- **Nutrition:** 2
- **Saturation:** 0.3
- **Effects:** None

**Identification:**
Fairy Ring Champignon is a small grassland mushroom that grows in fairy rings. Cap 2-5cm across, bell-shaped to flat with broad umbo, pale tan to buff. Gills white to cream, widely spaced, free. Stem 3-8cm tall, very tough and wiry (diagnostic), pale, smooth. Flesh thin, pale, with sweet smell.

**Culinary Value:** Good edible with sweet, nutty flavor. The tough stems must be discarded - only caps are eaten. Can be dried and powdered for seasoning.

**Lookalikes:**
- **Fool's Funnel** (Clitocybe rivulosa) - DEADLY. Crowded gills, no tough stem, different smell
- Various small brown mushrooms - Check for tough, wiry stem
