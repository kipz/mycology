### Garlic Parachute
*Marasmius scorodonius*

**Quick Reference:**
- **Item ID:** `mycology:collared_parachute`
- **Edibility:** Edible (used as flavoring)
- **Toxicity:** None
- **Season:** Summer to autumn (June-November)
- **Habitat:** Woodland floor, heathland

**In-Game Properties:**
- **Nutrition:** 1
- **Saturation:** 0.1
- **Effects:** None

**Identification:**
Tiny mushroom with strong garlic smell. Cap 0.5-2cm, convex, reddish-brown. Gills pale, widely spaced. Stem very thin, dark, tough. Smell of garlic is diagnostic.

Too small to eat as mushroom but dried and used as garlic substitute for seasoning.
