### Yellow Brain Fungus
*Tremella mesenterica*

**Quick Reference:**
- **Item ID:** `mycology:yellow_brain`
- **Edibility:** Edible (bland)
- **Toxicity:** None
- **Season:** Year-round (peak autumn-winter)
- **Habitat:** Dead hardwood branches

**In-Game Properties:**
- **Nutrition:** 1
- **Saturation:** 0.1
- **Effects:** None

**Identification:**
Bright yellow jelly fungus. Forms convoluted, brain-like masses 2-8cm across. Color bright golden-yellow. Texture gelatinous when wet, hard when dry (revives when moistened). Grows on dead attached branches.

Edible but flavorless. Used in Asian cuisine for texture. The bright yellow brain-like appearance is unmistakable.
