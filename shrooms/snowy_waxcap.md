### Snowy Waxcap
*Cuphophyllus virgineus*

**Quick Reference:**
- **Item ID:** `mycology:snowy_waxcap`

**Identification:**
Pure white waxcap. Cap 2-6cm, convex to flat, white, smooth, slightly greasy. Gills white, decurrent, waxy. Stem white. Grows in grassland.

Edible but mild flavor, not highly rated. Indicator of quality grassland.
