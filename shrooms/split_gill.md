### Split Gill
*Schizophyllum commune*

**Quick Reference:**
- **Item ID:** `mycology:split_gill`
- **Edibility:** Edible (chewy texture)
- **Toxicity:** None
- **Season:** Year-round
- **Habitat:** Dead hardwood

**In-Game Properties:**
- **Nutrition:** 1
- **Saturation:** 0.1
- **Effects:** None

**Identification:**
Small fan-shaped bracket. Brackets 1-4cm, white to grey, hairy upper surface. **Diagnostic:** Gills appear split lengthwise (not true gills but modified). Extremely common on dead branches worldwide.

Edible in some Asian cuisines but very tough and chewy. Usually dried first.
